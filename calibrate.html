Find your Focus Window.

No goals. No pressure. Just you, a task, and a timer. This takes as long as your focus does.

How calibration works

1
Pick a skill below and start the timer.
2
Do the task for real — read, meditate, whatever you choose.
3
The moment you feel bored, distracted, or restless — press SPACEBAR (or tap the button on mobile).
4
We calculate 80% of that time as your daily goal — a target you can always hit, even on bad days.
💡

Why 80%? Your calibrated window is the edge of comfort. Your daily goal should feel easy on hard days and natural on good ones. You'll naturally exceed it — and that's the point.
